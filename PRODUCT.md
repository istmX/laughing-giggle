# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Builders, small teams, and solo developers using Zenix to sign in, create an account, and access the main app experience. They are likely working at a desk or on the go and need the auth flow to feel fast, clear, and low-friction on both desktop and mobile.

## Product Purpose

Zenix is a product workspace for organizing context around software work. The auth surface exists to get people into the app quickly while reinforcing clarity, trust, and a polished product feel.

## Brand Personality

Focused, modern, trustworthy.

The experience should feel calm and competent, with a little visual polish, but never distract from the task of logging in or creating an account.

## Anti-references

Avoid decorative SaaS fluff, over-animated auth screens, heavy glassmorphism, crowded forms, and novelty controls that make standard login/signup tasks feel unfamiliar.

## Design Principles

- Make the task obvious immediately.
- Use familiar product patterns so the interface disappears into the workflow.
- Preserve clarity across screen sizes and input methods.
- Keep hierarchy tight and purposeful.
- Add visual polish only where it improves confidence or comprehension.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA contrast and interaction clarity. Support keyboard navigation, visible focus states, readable text sizes on small screens, and layouts that remain usable at mobile widths without requiring precision tapping.
