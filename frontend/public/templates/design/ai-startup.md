# AI Startup Landing Page — Design System Spec

## Colors
- **Canvas**: `#030303` (pure dark)
- **Ink**: `#ffffff` (white)
- **Accent Primary**: `#10b981` (emerald green)
- **Accent Primary Glow**: `rgba(16,185,129,0.08)`
- **Border**: `#18181b` (stone gray)

## Typography
- **Headings**: Inter weight `800` (heavy display)
- **Body copy**: Inter weight `320`/`330` (clean, high contrast)
- **Letter Spacing**: `-0.02em` on displays, `normal` on body

## Motion
- **Reveals**: Framer Motion spring fade-in staggers completion target `400ms`.
- **FAQ transitions**: Expand and collapse elements inside `200ms` with cubic ease.
